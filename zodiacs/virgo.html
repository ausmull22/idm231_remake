Just like the little ones you love, Vanilla Life Cereal tastes simply sweet and is totally irresistible. Life is a breakfast cereal made by the Quaker Oats Company. It was formerly made of oats, soy protein concentrate, sodium casienate and sugar, but now also contains corn flour, whole wheat flour, and rice flour. Introduced in 1961, the cereal is distinguished by its characteristic brown checkered square pattern.
