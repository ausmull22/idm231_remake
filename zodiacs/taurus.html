Raspberry Red, Lemony Lemon, Orangey Orange, Wildberry Blue, Grapity Purple, And Watermelon! When Trix launched in 1954, it was the first fruit-flavored cereal that was colorful and fun. While Trix cereal has evolved since their initial launch, the Punchline has remained unchanged: Silly rabbit, Trix are for kids!
